Safety Events Dashboard

Select a safety data metric from the dropdown menu, and this chart will show how that metric's average value has changed over the months. The data is grouped monthly, and a rolling average is applied to smooth out short-term variations, allowing you to spot trends and patterns more easily. For example, you can choose metrics like: - Transit Vehicle Rider Fatalities - People Waiting or Leaving Fatalities - Transit Vehicle Operator Fatalities - and many more... Explore the safety data and uncover insights by changing the selection.
